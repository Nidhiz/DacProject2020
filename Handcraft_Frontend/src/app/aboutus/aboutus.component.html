Celebrating An Unparallele Heritage

Driven By a Belief

We hope to be able to raise the dignity of the Indian artisan in our own

small way. It is our effort to offer an undeniably unique selection of

traditional art and craft translated into a range of attractive contemporary

products, while ensuring a fair price for every single artisan whose creations

have been featured on this portal, while bringing it closer to people across

the world. We endeavour to support our artisans not just by promoting

what they make, but also by helping them with ideas and designs to

innovate and create relevant craft products that appeal to changing tastes

and modern preferences, to help them find wider markets.
